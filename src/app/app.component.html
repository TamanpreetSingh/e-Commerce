Standing Work Desk

WORKS WITH YOUR HEIGHT

Goes from 29 to 47.5 inches (74 to 121 cm) to give you the full experience of a sit-stand desk. You can set this standing desk converter according to your height and use it as a full-time standing desk OR move between sitting and standing positions throughout your work-day.
Urbo® Wilg means business and brings space, solidity and size to your workspace. The work surface is large enough to accommodate laptops, monitors, digital accessories, paperwork, etc. It is a free-standing desk that allows you maximum movement. Steel desk is built for dependable support.
